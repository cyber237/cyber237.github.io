Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Agile Development is a software methodology, whereas Scrum is one of process frameworks that follows Agile.

Both Sprints of Scrum and Iterations of Iterative Incremental model deliver working product increments. However, these differ in that:

  • Lifecycles of Sprint and Iteration are different.
  • Sprints are time-boxed, while Iterations are not.
  • Duration of Sprints is much less compared to durations of Iterations.

Scrum Master is a role that someone with a job title fills. Normal practice is that the person playing the role of project manager plays the ScrumMaster’s role as well.
